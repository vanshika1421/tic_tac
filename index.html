How to Play Tic Tac Toe Pro

Objective

Be the first player to get 3 of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

How to Play

  1. Players take turns placing their marks (X or O) on the 3x3 grid
  2. Player X always goes first
  3. Click on any empty square to place your mark
  4. First to get 3 in a row wins!
  5. If all squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw

Keyboard Shortcuts

1-9 Place mark on board position
R Reset current game
Ctrl+U Undo last move
Ctrl+S Open settings
Esc Close menus

AI Difficulty Levels

Easy: AI makes random moves with occasional strategy
Medium: Balanced strategic play with smart blocking
Hard: Optimal play using advanced algorithms
